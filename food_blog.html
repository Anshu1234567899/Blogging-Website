Yummy Pasta

How to make pasta.....?

Step 1: Cook the pasta

Boil water: In a large pot, bring water to a boil. Add a generous amount of salt to the water (this helps flavor the pasta). Cook the pasta: Once the water is boiling, add your pasta and cook according to the package instructions, usually 8-10 minutes, or until it's al dente (tender but still firm to the bite). Drain the pasta: Once cooked, drain the pasta, reserving about ¬Ω cup of pasta water (you can use this to adjust the sauce later). Set asid.

You can also make the sauce in advance and refrigerate it for later use.
